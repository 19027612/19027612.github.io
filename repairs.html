Here are simple steps to try and repair your computer

DO NOT TRY THESE STEPS IF YOUR COMPUTER IS DOING AN UPDATE/DOWNLOAD!

Try turning your computer off and on

If an app is slowing down your computer open task manager and find the application and click "End Task"

Check for updates for your Operating System and the Application that is causing the problem(s)

If you are still having problems with your computer please follow these steps to organise a repair

  1. Make sure you have completed the steps shown above correctly
  2. Head to the contact page Here
  3. Fill in the contact form and provide as much information as possible
  4. Wait for 1-2 business days for a reply with either a solution to your problem or a slot for you to bring in the computer to have it looked at by one of our specialists
  5. On the day of your repair please arrive with time to spare as there can be delays
  6. Please be patient as repairs can take a while to be completed
